Data Sources & Information

This map visualizes combined vulnerability in Barcelona based on multiple factors:

1. Combined Heat (UHI + LST): An average index derived from the Urban Heat Island (UHI) effect and Land Surface Temperature (LST) data from Google Earth Engine (2021 to 2024).

2. Drought (SPEI): Standardized Precipitation Evapotranspiration Index, indicating hydrological stress from Google Earth Engine (2023).

3. Vulnerable Population: Data on specific vulnerable demographic groups (e.g., population aged 65+) from census 2011.

4. Immigrants: Distribution of immigrant population across neighborhoods from Datos Barcelona (2024).

5. Low Income (Inverse): Areas with lower income levels from Datos Barcelona (2024).

All data points are aggregated to local neighborhood areas and normalized to create a composite vulnerability score (0-1).
